CAESAR MOD

This mod was made in honor of Julius Caesar and
as a sign of appreciation towards modders of the
original Greek Mod to support their work, in the
hope that the game will become popular again all
over the world and receive the treatment it deserves
by being the best strategy game ever: Ave Caesar!

You are free to use my mod as a starting point
for your projects, I ask you just the courtesy
of indicating my name in the credits/thanks
(see LICENSE file for more information).
